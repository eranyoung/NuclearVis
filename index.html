Description

This project was inspired by the 2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine and the resulting increased threat of nuclear warfare. NuclearVis is a visualization dashboard made up of multiple types of visualizations which aim to illustrate the growth and danger of increasing nuclear power throughout history into the modern day.
